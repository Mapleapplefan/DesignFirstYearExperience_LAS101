INQUIRE
LAS 101 was a very unique course that I was not expecting to come into UIUC. However, it has proven to be a very fun, interactive, and wholesome class that does its job of being an LAS orientation correctly. Although I believe there was a bit of busy work, most of the homework is manageable and the quizzes are well made. I believe that the course did a great job in introducing RSO’s, opportunities, and fun things to do on campus. I came into LAS 101 with a couple questions on friendship and healthcare, both of which have been answered. In addition, I was challenged to step outside of my comfort zone and go check out random things on campus. What I liked most about this course was that as well as the amazing plethora of students that I got to meet. I learned about a lot of different majors and the class itself was one of the most diverse I’ve seen.
